2D Physics Engine

C++
SDL
This project was for my Bachelor thesis where I went on a hands-on adventure into the world of custom 2D physics engines, with a focus on determinism, optimization, and realistic collision systems.

In this project I dove deep into how all these elements come together for a physics engine that works seamlessly.

  • Custom collision detection/resolution for circles, boxes and convex polygons
  • Particle physics
  • Bounding boxes/circles
  • Determenistic result
