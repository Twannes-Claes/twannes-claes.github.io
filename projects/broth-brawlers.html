Broth Brawlers

Unity
C#
Perforce
Team
Broth Brawlers is a small 3D couch party game. Made as Game Project by a group of students at DAE Howest.

You play as a bowl filled with juicy ramen noodles. As cute they might look, the bowls are dangerous! They carry sharp chopsticks as weapon, these can be used for jousting the other players in the arena. Nasty! Push the other players around and make them spill soup and noodles, or throw them out! Every player can move, turn, dash and spill soup at will to make others slip. Jousting can be done after charging your attack for long enough. The spilled soup makes you slip but also increases your soup amount as you pick it up.

The player with the most noodles left when the timers ends, wins the game!

I was the programmer in this project, so all the game logic you encounter in this project was done by me, even audio.

Here is a list of my implementations in this project:

  • Player movement
  • Game Mechanics
  • Lobby loading
  • Level interactables
  • Co-op system
  • UI Programming
  • Audio logic
