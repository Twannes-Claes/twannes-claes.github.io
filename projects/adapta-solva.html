Adapta Solva

Unity
C#
Perforce
Team
Adapta Solva is a recent group project that I had the opportunity to work on for six months. As part of the team, I took on the majority of the programming work, gaining valuable experience in a professional group setting where communication and collaboration were key. This project allowed me to not only develop my technical skills, but also learn how to effectively work together with a team towards a common goal.

Here is a list of a few of my implementations in this project:

  • Interactable item/treasure system
  • Transparent camera dithering
  • Custom player/input movement system
  • Wall climbing ability
  • Door/Button interactions
  • Optimizing game performance
